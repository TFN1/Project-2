Services

At Performance edge physio

Our advanced physical therapy approach and specialized diagnostic services help you quickly recover from pain or injury. Whether you have been suffering from chronic pain, or just suffered a recent injury, our physical therapy specialists can quickly discover the root cause of your pain, and provide cutting-edge hands-on treatments to speed up your recovery.

Sports Physiotherapy

Sport-specific rehabilitation to help get you back on the field sooner, performing at your best.

General Physiotherapy

General and Post-Operative Physiotherapy to get you back to the game of life as quickly as possible.

1-on-1 and Group Pilates

1-on-1 and Group Pilates 6 week programs run by Matwork Qualified Pilates

Injury Prevention Screening

Sport-Specific musculoskeletal screening to help with injury prevention and performance enhancement.

Massage Therapy

PEP has qualified Massage Therapists for all your massage needs.

Dry Needling

Our Physiotherapists are qualified Dry Needling practitioners (including Advanced Dry Needling).

Hydrotherapy

Our Physiotherapists provide private supervised hydrotherapy sessions. We also make individualised hydrotherapy programs based a comprehensive assessment.

Running Assessments

We have Running Assessment qualified Physios who use slow-motion video analysis to perform a gait check.

Our passion

At Performance Edge Physio, we are not just about the treatment of symptoms, we are committed to interventions that identify the cause of your injury. Once identified we will prescribe appropriate treatment and rehabilitation program, which may include a combination of hands on treatment such as mobilisation/manipulation, soft tissue mobilisation, movement control retraining, specific rehabilitation and importantly a home exercise program.
